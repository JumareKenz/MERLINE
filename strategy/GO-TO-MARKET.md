## 3. Sales Process

### 3.1 Sales Funnel

```
Lead → Discovery → Demo → Trial → Evaluation → Procurement → Onboarding → Go-Live

Stage 1: Lead Generation
├── Inbound (website, content, referrals)
├── Outbound (targeted outreach to M&E Directors)
├── Partner referrals (consultants, implementing orgs)
├── Events (MERL conferences, sector gatherings)
└── DONOR (when donor mandates platform)
        ↓
Stage 2: Discovery (1-2 calls)
├── Understand org structure, study volume, current tools, pain points
├── Identify decision-making unit and budget
├── Map Merline to their workflow
└── Qualification: budget, authority, need, timeline
        ↓
Stage 3: Demo (1 session)
├── Tailored to prospect's specific use case
├── Show end-to-end: design → collect → analyze → report
├── Highlight AI features and offline capability
└── Address compliance/security questions
        ↓
Stage 4: Free Trial (14-30 days)
├── Dedicated onboarding call
├── Import their indicator template (white-glove)
├── 1 form designed with their content
└── Weekly check-in calls
        ↓
Stage 5: Evaluation
├── Technical evaluation (security, integration, compliance)
├── User acceptance testing with 3-5 users
├── ROI calculation: time saved, cost reduction, quality improvement
└── Reference calls with existing customers
        ↓
Stage 6: Procurement (2-12 weeks)
├── Pricing proposal
├── Security questionnaire / SOC 2 report
├── DPA / contract negotiation
└── PO or payment
        ↓
Stage 7: Onboarding (2-4 weeks)
├── Organization setup
├── Admin training (1 session)
├── Researcher training (2 sessions)
├── Enumerator training (field-tested, in-app guidance)
└── First study launched on platform
        ↓
Stage 8: Go-Live
├── First data collected
├── Health check at 1 week, 1 month, 3 months
├── Customer success manager assigned
└── NPS survey at 30 days
```

## 6. Donor & Funding Strategy

### 6.1 Donor Engagement Approach

1. **Top-down**: Engage major donors (USAID, FCDO, Gates) to adopt Merline for their grantees/implementers.
2. **Bottom-up**: Existing grantees adopt Merline → donors see the data quality improvement → donors mandate.
3. **Partnership**: Donor-funded innovation grants to build donor-specific features.

### 6.2 Donor Value Proposition

| Donor Pain | Merline Solution |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Inconsistent data across grantees | Standardized indicators, forms, reporting templates |
| Late, poor-quality reports | Real-time dashboard access during implementation |
| Cannot compare across programs | Standardized indicator library, cross-portfolio views |
| High M&E cost burden | 50-70% reduction in reporting time for grantees |
| No institutional memory | Evidence repository persists across funding cycles |

### 7.1 Core Positioning

**For organizations that need to collect, analyze, and act on evidence, Merline is the AI-native MERL operating system that replaces 5-8 disconnected tools with one intelligent platform.**

### 7.2 Messaging by Segment

| Segment | Headline | Key Message |

| **Donors** | "Evidence you can trust. From collection to decision." | Standardized data, real-time access, auditable quality. |
| **Government** | "Your data. Your rules. Your sovereignty." | Local hosting, compliance certified, national-scale. |

### 7.3 Key Differentiators (Marketing Copy)

- **The first AI-native MERL platform** — Not a chatbot bolted on. AI designed into every workflow.
- **Offline-first, not offline-fallback** — Built for the field, not the office.
- **One platform, end-to-end** — Design → Collect → Analyze → Report → Learn. Replaces 5-8 tools.
- **Scientific rigor by design** — Methodologically sound workflows that prevent common research errors.
- **Enterprise-grade from day one** — Multi-tenant, schema-isolated, GDPR/SOC 2 designed.

## 9. Customer Success & Retention Strategy

### 9.1 Customer Health Scoring

| Metric | Weight | Green | Yellow | Red |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|-----|
| Login frequency (past 30 days) | 20% | > 15 days | 5-15 days | < 5 days |
| Submissions (past 30 days) | 20% | > 500 | 100-500 | < 100 |
| Support tickets (past 30 days) | 15% | 0-2 | 3-5 | > 5 |
| NPS score | 15% | > 40 | 20-40 | < 20 |
| Feature adoption (# of modules used) | 15% | > 5 | 3-5 | < 3 |
| Payment status | 15% | Current | < 30 days overdue | > 30 days overdue |

### 9.2 Retention Playbook

| Risk Signal | Action | Owner |
|------------|--------|-------|
| Low login (yellow) | Email campaign: new features, tips, webinars | Marketing |
| Low login (red) | Phone call: "What's blocking you?" | Customer Success |
| Low submissions | Check study status; proactive training | Customer Success |
| High support tickets | Trend analysis; root cause fix | Engineering + CS |
| Negative NPS | Personal follow-up within 24 hours | CEO / Product |
| Payment overdue | Automated reminders → personal call | Finance + CS |

### 9.3 Expansion Revenue Drivers

| Driver | Description | Expected Impact |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|

| **User growth** | Department → Organization → Multi-country | 3x account expansion |
| **Integration needs** | Pure platform → Connected ecosystem | 20% integration upsell |
