# Merline Platform Vision

## Mission

To become the world's leading AI-native Monitoring, Evaluation, Research and Learning (MERL) operating system.

## Core Identity

Merline is a scientifically rigorous, AI-native, enterprise-grade platform that transforms how organizations design research, collect data, generate evidence, and make decisions.

## Platform Pillars

| Pillar | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Scientific Rigor | Every workflow follows internationally accepted MERL methodologies |
| AI-Native | Intelligence is embedded into every workflow, not bolted on |
| Enterprise-Grade | Multi-tenant, secure, compliant, auditable, scalable |
| Human-Centered | Software disappears; researchers focus on their work |
| Offline-First | Field operations continue without connectivity |
| Globally Scalable | Supports organizations from small NGOs to governments |
| Extensible | API-first, plugin architecture, ecosystem-ready |
| Explainable | Every AI recommendation is grounded and traceable |

## Target Users

- Researchers and Evaluators
- Field Enumerators and Supervisors
- Program Managers and Monitoring Officers
- Government Agencies and Donors
- NGOs and International Development Organizations
- Healthcare and Public Health Institutions
- Academic Research Institutions

## Strategic North Star

By 2031, Merline should be the default platform for any organization that needs to collect, analyze, learn from, and act on evidence — from village-level surveys to national-scale programs.
