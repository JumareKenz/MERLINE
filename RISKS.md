|----|------|------------|--------|------------|

| R-008 | Executive agent coordination overhead slows progress | Medium | Medium | CEO orchestrates; clear handoff protocols |

| R-010 | Competitors (DevResults, ActivityInfo, TolaData) already established | Medium | Medium | Differentiate through AI-native + offline-first + scientific rigor |

## Risk Response Strategy

- **Critical** (High/High): Immediate mitigation required
- **Major** (High/Medium or Medium/High): Active monitoring with response plan
- **Minor** (Low/Medium or Medium/Low): Periodic review
