# Merline Training Program

## Training Philosophy

Training builds confidence, not dependency. Every user should leave training able to perform their core workflows independently. Training is not a one-time event — it is a continuous capability-building journey from new user to power user to organizational champion.

**Core principles:**
- Role-based: each persona gets exactly what they need
- Hands-on: learning by doing, not watching
- Contextual: training uses the customer's real data and workflows
- Measurable: completion rates, assessment scores, and on-the-job performance
- Iterative: continuous improvement based on feedback

## Training Delivery Methods

### Method 1: Self-Paced Online

**Platform:** Merline Learning Hub (integrated into platform knowledge base)

**Content Types:**
- Video tutorials (3-7 min each)
- Interactive walkthroughs (simulated platform with guidance)
- Written guides (step-by-step with screenshots)
- Knowledge base articles (reference)
- Quick-reference cards (printable PDF)

**Best for:** Administrators, experienced researchers, refresher training, independent learners

**Availability:** 24/7, any device

| Content | Count | Avg Duration |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| Getting Started videos | 5 | 3 min |
| Role-specific tutorial videos | 8-12 per role | 5 min |
| Interactive walkthroughs | 6 | 10 min |
| Written guides | 15 | 3 pages |
| Quick-reference cards | 8 | 2 pages |

### Method 2: Live Virtual (Instructor-Led)

**Platform:** Zoom / Teams with screen sharing and breakout rooms

**Structure:**
- Presentation: 25% of time
- Live demonstration: 25% of time
- Hands-on practice in sandbox: 40% of time
- Q&A: 10% of time

**Best for:** New users, complex workflows, groups needing real-time support

**Group size:** 5-15 participants ideal

**Schedule:**
- Admin/Researcher: 4 sessions (2 hrs each)
- Enumerator: 1 session (3 hrs)
- Supervisor: 2 sessions (2 hrs each)
- Manager: 1 session (1.5 hrs)

### Method 3: In-Person

**Best for:** Enumerator training in field locations, large group onboarding, organizations with low digital literacy

**Structure:**
- Day 1: Admin + Researcher training (6 hrs)
- Day 2: Enumerator training (4 hrs) + Supervisor training (3 hrs)
- Day 3: Manager briefing (2 hrs) + Go-live preparation (2 hrs)

**Materials needed:**
- Projector and screen
- Printed training manuals
- Quick-reference cards
- Test devices (if enumerators don't have their own)

### Method 4: Blended

**Recommended approach for most customers.**

| Week | Method | Audience |
|------|--------|----------|
| Pre-onboarding | Self-paced: Getting Started videos | All users |
| Week 1 | Live virtual: Admin setup + Platform overview | Admin, Researcher |
| Week 2 | Live virtual: Questionnaire design | Researcher, M&E |
| Week 3 | In-person: Enumerator training + Supervisor training | Enumerator, Supervisor |
| Week 3 | Live virtual: Manager briefing | Manager, Executive |
| Week 4 | Self-paced: refresher + Reference guides | All users |
| Day 30 | Live virtual: Advanced features | Power users |

## AI Training (Phase 2)

### How to Work with AI Recommendations

| Module | Content | Duration | Audience |
|--------|---------|----------|----------|
| Understanding AI in Merline | What AI does, how it works, confidence scores, reasoning traces | 20 min | All users |
| AI Research Design Assistant | Using AI to suggest ToC, LogFrame, indicators; reviewing and accepting/rejecting suggestions | 30 min | Researcher |
| AI Questionnaire Assistant | AI-suggested questions, skip logic, validation; editing AI output | 30 min | Researcher |
| AI Data Quality Engine | Understanding quality scores, anomaly detection, fraud indicators | 20 min | Supervisor |
| AI Report Writer | Using AI to generate narrative, executive summaries; review and edit | 30 min | Manager/Researcher |
| AI Insight Engine (Phase 2+) | Natural language queries, AI-generated insights, data storytelling | 20 min | Manager/Executive |
| AI Best Practices | When to trust AI, when to override, how to provide feedback | 15 min | All users |
| AI Limitations & Ethics | Hallucination awareness, bias considerations, data privacy | 15 min | All users |

### AI Training Principles
- **Transparency first**: Users must understand what AI is doing and why
- **Human-in-the-loop**: Always review before accepting AI output
- **Confidence awareness**: Teach users to check confidence scores and citations
- **Feedback loop**: Every AI interaction improves the model; teach users to provide feedback

## Training Metrics

### Measurement Framework

| Metric | Definition | Target | Measurement |
|--------|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Training completion rate | % of enrolled users who complete all required modules | > 85% | Training platform analytics |
| Knowledge assessment pass rate | % of users scoring > 80% on first attempt | > 80% | Assessment system |
| Time to competency | Days from training start to independent workflow completion | < 5 days | Platform analytics |
| Training CSAT | Post-training satisfaction (1-5) | > 4.5/5 | Post-training survey |
| Certification rate | % of trained users who obtain certification | > 60% | Certification records |
| Refresher adherence | % of certified users completing refresher within validity period | > 70% | Training platform |

### Reporting Dashboard

Training metrics are reported in the Customer Success dashboard with:
- Completion rate by role (per customer)
- Assessment pass rate by module
- Training CSAT trend
- Certification status per user
- Upcoming recertification dates

## Train-the-Trainer Program

### Purpose
Enable large organizations (100+ users, multiple country offices) to deliver their own Merline training, reducing dependency on the Merline team and accelerating adoption across distributed teams.

### Program Structure

| Phase | Content | Duration | Format |
|-------|---------|----------|--------|
| Phase 1: Foundation | Complete all role certifications (Admin, Researcher, Supervisor, Enumerator) | Self-paced | Online |
| Phase 2: Training Skills | Adult learning principles, training delivery techniques, handling difficult questions | 1 day | Live virtual |
| Phase 3: Co-Delivery | Co-deliver 2 training sessions with Merline trainer, receive feedback | 2 sessions | Live |
| Phase 4: Certification Assessment | Deliver 1 training session independently, observed by Merline trainer | 1 session | Live |
| Phase 5: Ongoing Support | Quarterly trainer community calls, updated materials, trainer forum | Ongoing | Community |

### Trainer Toolkit

| Resource | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Trainer's Manual | Complete session plans, timing, activities, talking points |
| Slide Decks | Customizable presentation for each role track |
| Exercise Workbooks | Hands-on exercises for each module |
| Assessment Bank | Pre- and post-training assessments |
| Quick-Reference Cards | Printable role-specific cards |
| Training Environment | Dedicated sandbox for practice |
| FAQ Document | Common questions and answers |
| Training Log Template | Track attendance, completion, assessment scores |

### Trainer Qualification Criteria

| Criteria | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| Experience with platform | 2+ months active use |
| Role certifications | All applicable certifications |
| Training skills assessment | Pass observed training delivery |
| Language proficiency | Training delivery language |
| Time commitment | Available for minimum 2 training sessions/month |

## Training Environment
